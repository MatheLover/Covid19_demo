About

This site allows users to make queries about COVID 19 and other relevant data. The information are from Our World in Data.

Home
In Home, users can query COVID 19 disease statistics about their chosen country or region during a specified period.

COVID 19 Statistics Mapping
In COVID 19 Statistics Mapping Section, users can choose either to view daily(on a specific day) disease statistics or cumulative(during a period of time) disease statistics upon a real map. In particular, a choropleth map with time slider will be generated to visualize changes in disease statistics over the specified period.

Public Health Authority Response
In addition, number of COVID 19 tests, vaccinations, and vaccinated population should be evaluated to assess the effectiveness of public health authority response.

Social Economic Factor
Social economic situations may also affect COVID 19. In this section, users are encouraged to examine the relationship between socio -economic factors and COVID 19. The socioeconomic factors include the followings:
  1. Stringency index: A composite indicator reflecting the scale of closures (e.g., school, workplace) ranging from 0 to 100(the higher the value, the more stringent the response)
  2. Percentage of population with 65 or above in one country or region
  3. Percentage of population with 70 or above in one country or region
  4. GDP per capita: Gross Domestic Product per capita based on 2011 data


Public Health Statistics
Health statistics about the overall population may also affect the COVID 19 situation. People with cardiovascular disease or diabetes are much more likely to get infected with the COVID 19 virus. In this section, users explore whether countries with more populations suffering from cardiovascular diseases or diabetes are under greater COVID 19 threats. The health statistics include the followings:
  1. Cardiovascular death rate: Number of deaths per 100,000 on a yearly basis
  2. Diabetes prevalence: % of population with diabetes aged from 20 to 79


Public Health Facility
Public health facility is likely to influence the severity of COVID 19 in a country or region. In this section, users are encounraged to explore whether countries with more health facilities have less severe COVID 19 situations. The public health facility category includes the followings:
  1. Hand-washing Facility:Proportion of population who can access basic hand-washing facilities
  2. Hospital Beds per Thousand: The number of hospital beds every 1000 people can access
